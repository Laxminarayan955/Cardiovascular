Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this website about?

This website provides cardiovascular risk analysis based on your health data to help assess potential heart health risks.

2. How does the risk analysis work?

The analysis is based on medical research and considers factors such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, lifestyle habits, and other health conditions.

3. Is the risk analysis accurate?

The results are based on established medical guidelines and data-driven analysis, but they should not replace professional medical advice.

4. What type of data do I need to provide?

You will need to enter information such as your age, weight, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, smoking habits, and medical history.

5. Is my personal health data safe?

Yes, we prioritize user privacy and follow industry best practices to secure your data.

6. Will my data be shared with anyone?

No, your personal health data is confidential and will not be shared without your consent.

7. How can I check my cardiovascular risk?

Simply sign up, enter your health details, and receive a personalized risk analysis report.

8. Can I book an appointment with a doctor through this site?

Currently, our site only provides cardiovascular risk analysis. However, we recommend consulting a healthcare professional for further evaluation.

9. How often should I check my cardiovascular risk?

You can check periodically, especially if there are changes in your lifestyle or health conditions.

10. What should I do if my risk level is high?

If your risk is high, we recommend consulting a doctor for further medical advice and lifestyle changes.

11. Does this website provide medical treatment?

No, we only provide risk analysis based on your health data. Always seek professional medical guidance for treatment.

12. Can I improve my cardiovascular health based on the results?

Yes, the site may suggest general health tips, but for personalized medical advice, consult a doctor.
